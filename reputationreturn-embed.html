Frequently asked questions
Is it really free?
Yes — completely free, no credit card and no expiring trial. Enter a name, pick your platforms, and get a full PDF reputation report.
How accurate is the reputation score?
The score is generated by AI analysing real social posts, comments, reviews, news coverage and web search results — blending social sentiment with web & search presence.
Do I need to create an account?
No. You can generate and download a full report without signing up. We only ask for an email so we can send you a copy of the PDF.
Which platforms do you scan?
X (Twitter), Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn and Google Business — plus a web & search intelligence layer that pulls in news, reviews and press mentions.
Is my search private?
Yes. We only analyse publicly available information — the same posts, reviews and results anyone could find. We never post, never access private accounts, and never share your data.
